Quick setup guide

Required for iPhone AI to work. Do this once:

1. Open your Google Sheet →
2. Click Extensions → Apps Script
3. Delete any existing code, paste the full contents of cellar-proxy.gs
4. Click Deploy → New deployment
5. Type: Web App · Execute as: Me · Access: Anyone
6. Click Deploy → copy the Web App URL
7. Paste URL in the field above → tap Save → tap Test

Once connected, all AI calls route through your Sheet. Works on every browser and iPhone. Your API key stays private — never in the HTML file.

Your wines stay in your Google account. Cellar. stores nothing externally.
